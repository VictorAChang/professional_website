I believe in making a positive impact in the world through my actions and support for meaningful causes. Christ guides my faith and checks my actions every day. It is important to love one another and ensure that we care more about each other than ourselves. Here are some of the key areas I am passionate about:

Finding a Cure for Cancer

As a thyroid cancer survivor, I am passionate about supporting research, awareness, and organizations dedicated to finding cures and helping those affected by cancer.

Fighting Disparity

I am committed to fighting against economic and social disparity, especially in third world countries, by supporting initiatives that provide opportunities, resources, and hope to those in need.

Faith in Christ & Spreading Love

My faith in Christ is the foundation of my life and inspires me to spread love, hope, and compassion to every single person, regardless of background or circumstance.

Fighting Human Trafficking & Child Rights

I actively support efforts to end human trafficking and defend human rights, especially for children, by raising awareness and backing organizations that protect the vulnerable.
